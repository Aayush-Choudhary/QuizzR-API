Ever wanted to Build a Quiz Game but gave up at the prospect of Hunting for the Right Questions and set them up?
Well, look no further, the QuizzR API has got your back!

QuizzR is an Open-Source RESTful API to return General Knowledge questions at Random or by Difficulty.

Want to Add Your Questions?

Contribution Guidelines:

  • Add your Questions here.
    Keep the id unique by incrementing the value of yours by 1 of the previous.
  • Keep the Questions Global Centric and not about a particular region.
    Eg: Who was the Vice-President of India in 1980? is NOT a global-centric question.
    Keep the Questions diverse.
  • We understand that difficulty is subjective and each person may have different parameters for the same.
    Hence, keep the difficulty to what you expect for an average person.
